Our Story

RoshaClub is not just a gym—it's a legacy. A place where dreams, dedication, and determination turned into a world-class fitness haven.

The Humble Beginnings

It all started in a small garage in the heart of our city, where four passionate fitness enthusiasts—**Roman Rochniak**, **Georgiy Danilov**, **Mikita Bakhai**, and **Vasyl Hrytsyshyn**—decided to turn their vision into reality. With nothing but a few rusty dumbbells and a shared dream, they transformed the space into a hub for fitness and community.

From Garage to Glory

Over the years, the garage became a beacon of excellence, attracting not only local enthusiasts but also global icons. Legends like **Arnold Schwarzenegger**, **Kevin Levrone**, **Ronnie Coleman**, and **Tom Platz** trained here, inspired by the authenticity and relentless drive of the founders.
